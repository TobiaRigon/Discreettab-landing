DiscreetTab Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2024

DiscreetTab is a personal hobby project by Tobia Rigon. The extension saves its settings only inside Chrome Sync so you can use them on your own devices. No external servers, analytics, or tracking scripts are involved.

What is stored

Chrome Sync keeps track of the domains you mark as discreet, your alias choices, and whether the extension is enabled. This information never leaves your Google account and is not shared, sold, or processed elsewhere.

Data collection

I do not collect telemetry, crash reports, or any personal data. There is no advertising or monetisation; support is completely optional via the link on the landing page.
