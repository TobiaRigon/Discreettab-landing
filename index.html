Key features for discreet browsing

Unified Chrome Sync storage

All settings live inside Chrome Sync so your private list and aliases stay consistent on every device you sign into.

Instant MutationObserver updates

DiscreetTab reacts the moment titles change, keeping screen shares clean without polling loops or performance hits.

Keyboard-first control

Use the shortcut or toolbar icon to flip between normal and discreet states before someone glances at your screen.

Custom aliases

Give each domain a friendly label like “Finance” or “Support” so you know what is open even when the title is hidden.

Reduced-motion friendly

Animations respect system preferences, keeping the disguise comfortable for every user.

No analytics or ads

DiscreetTab is a hobby project with zero telemetry and no advertising code—your browsing stays on your device.

Frequently asked questions

Does DiscreetTab slow down Chrome?
No. It injects a lightweight observer only when you toggle discreet mode, keeping performance identical to normal browsing.
What information gets stored?
Only your discreet domain list, aliases, and whether the extension is enabled. Everything stays inside Chrome Sync.
Can I undo the disguise quickly?
Yes. Hit Ctrl+Shift+S again or click the icon to restore the original tab title and favicon immediately.
